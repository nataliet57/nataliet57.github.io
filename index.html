hey i'm natalie !

I’m a 17 year old passionate about photography, technology, and social justice. When I’m not too busy with school, I explore the diverse streets of Toronto. This humble metropolis is where I capture moments of my home city. My passion for coding began when I attended my first hackathon and learned about the potential of technology. From there, coding has become a hobby, and my curiosity for this field took off. I’m a human rights activist, fighting for social justice in our world in hopes of a brighter future. Most passionate about gun violence in North America, I hope to work with like minded individuals to find a solution to this ongoing tragedy.
